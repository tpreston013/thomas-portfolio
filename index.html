About Me

Thomas is an alumni from the University of Central Arkansas, where he studied in the school of Communication. He graduated with a bachelor of arts degree in Public Relations with a minor in Marketing. He is currently enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp through the University of Washington. After completing the course in March, he plans to pursue a career in web development in the Seattle area.

Thomas grew up in a small town called Smackover. In high school, he began his love for design as a member of the yearbook staff for four years, and served as Editor his senior year. He currently lives in the great city of Seattle with his partner. In his free time, Thomas enjoys watching horror movies, reading Stephen King books, and spending time with his partner and friends.
